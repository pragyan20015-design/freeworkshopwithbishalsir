About Me

My journey, mission, and what drives me to teach

BR

Bishal Rijal

Full-Stack Developer & Programming Instructor

I'm a passionate developer and educator based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Over the years, I've combined my love for coding with a strong belief that quality education should be accessible to everyone — which is why I host free programming workshops alongside my personal classes.

I specialize in helping students go from zero to building real, working software they can be proud of.

🎯 My Mission

To break down the barriers that keep people from learning to code. Programming changed my life, and I want to help others discover the same opportunities — regardless of their background or budget.

💡 My Approach

I believe in learning by building. Every lesson combines clear concepts with hands-on practice, so students not only understand the "what" but also the "why" and "how" behind every line of code.

My Journey

Started Programming

Discovered the world of code

Web Development

Mastered full-stack development

Teaching

Started mentoring students

Began offering personal classes to friends and peers, realizing my passion for explaining complex topics in simple ways.

Today

Free workshops & personal mentorship

Now hosting free community workshops and one-on-one classes, helping dozens of students build careers in tech.
